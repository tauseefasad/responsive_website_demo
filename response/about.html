About the website

The responsive website typically includes the following elements to ensure optimal viewing and functionality across different devices and screen sizes:
Mobile-First Approach: Designing and developing the website with a focus on mobile devices, ensuring it looks and functions well on small screens.
Fluid Layout: Using fluid grids and flexible units (such as percentages or relative units) to allow the content to adjust and resize smoothly based on the screen size
Navigation: Designing a navigation menu that is easily accessible and user-friendly on both desktop and mobile devices. This may involve implementing mobile-friendly menus, such as off-canvas or dropdown menus.
